After getting my Diploma, I wasn’t thinking of going to college, I was thinking of helping my father with his work and was thinking to open a business. But my father want me to become a better man, so I started my study in Benjamin Franklin Institute of Technology. I always love about computer and how software develop, so I begin my study with computer engineering technology.  I think I made the best choice of my life by going to college. After working 6 mouths in CVS, my manager promote me from a cashier to a merchandiser, with a rise of 8%. After going to school for one year, I took a semester off and work 65 hours a week to save for college tuition.

In January 2015, I started my study at Benjamin Franklin Institute of Technology to get my bachelor in Health information technology and along with the major, I took extra classes like Web design, Enterprise database management and other classes based with web and application development.
